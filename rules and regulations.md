# Wellbeing Ideathon - Rules and Regulations

---

## Judging Criteria

Projects will be evaluated on the following criteria:

| Criteria | Description |
|----------|-------------|
| **Relevance to Challenge** | Alignment with the provided mental health & wellbeing brief |
| **Originality & Creativity** | Uniqueness of the approach; fresh perspective on the issue |
| **Feasibility** | Practicality of implementation, considering current tech & resources |
| **Potential Impact** | Scope of positive effect on individuals, teams, or society |
| **Scalability** | Ability to adapt and grow beyond the initial concept stage |
| **Human-Centric Design** | Empathy, inclusivity, and user experience considerations |
| **Quality of Presentation** | Clarity, structure, and persuasiveness of pitch materials |

> Projects should be developed during the ideathon and presented in the form of a **Business Deck proposal** + **pre-recorded Video-pitch**.

---

## Concept Proposal - Business Deck

### Deck Structure (8–10 Slides)

1. **Title Slide**
   - Team name, project title, challenge, team members

2. **Problem**
   - What mental health or well-being issue are you solving? Why is it important?

3. **Solution**
   - Your idea in a nutshell — what makes it innovative or unique?

4. **Target Audience**
   - Who benefits and how? Include brief user insight

5. **How It Works**
   - Core features or process (visuals encouraged)

6. **Impact**
   - Expected outcomes and positive effects

7. **Feasibility & Scalability**
   - How it can be implemented and grown

8. **Team & Roles**
   - Who you are and what each person contributes

### Submission Reminder

- **Deadline:** December 4th (6 pm EST)
- **Format:** PDF or PowerPoint
- **Ensure alignment** with judging criteria (relevance, originality, feasibility, impact, scalability, human-centric design, presentation quality)

## Video Pitch

### Requirements

| Requirement | Details |
|-------------|---------|
| **Length** | 3 minutes maximum |
| **Format** | .MP4 or .MOV |
| **Orientation** | Horizontal |
| **Audio** | Clear audio required |

### Content to Cover

- **The Problem:** What real-world challenge are you solving?
- **The Idea:** What is your innovative solution?
- **The Impact:** Who will benefit, and how?
- **The Uniqueness:** Why is this idea different?
- **The Team:** Briefly introduce your group

### Style Guidelines

- **Inspiring, clear, and engaging** — aim to tell a story, not just list facts
- **Creativity encouraged:** Visuals, mock-ups, storytelling, and examples (no coding required)

### Submission

- **Upload by:** December 4th (6 pm EST)
