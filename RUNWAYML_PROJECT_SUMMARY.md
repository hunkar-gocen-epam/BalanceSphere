## üî• HOW IT WORKS (The Three Engines)

### **1. LOAD Engine: "Who's drowning?"**
- **What it sees:** Your calendar shows 38 hours of meetings (team average: 25). GitHub shows commits at 1 AM. Slack shows you're responding on weekends.
- **What it does:** Reaches out proactively: "Your PULSE score is 67/100‚Äîelevated for 3 weeks. Want to explore options?"
- **The magic:** It doesn't just alert. It offers help. "Block focus time? Draft a message to your manager? Use a recovery credit?"

### **2. REST Engine: "Can you actually rest?"**
- **The problem:** Maria has 15 unused PTO days. Not because she doesn't need rest‚Äîbecause last time she returned to 400 emails and three angry clients.
- **What it does:** Before vacation: auto-generates handoffs, delegates tasks, notifies stakeholders. During vacation: batches non-urgent items, logs decisions. On return: "5-minute briefing: 2 decisions made, 3 kudos, 1 question. Nothing's on fire."
- **The result:** Maria books another vacation in March.

### **3. BOND Engine: "Are you connected?"**
- **What it sees:** Alex hasn't attended a social event in 6 weeks. No informal chats. No virtual coffees. Isolation creeping in.
- **What it does:** Surfaces missed kudos. Suggests low-pressure connection opportunities. Matches for virtual coffee with people with shared interests.
- **Why it matters:** Prevents isolation before it becomes loneliness.
