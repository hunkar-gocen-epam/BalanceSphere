## üé¨ RUNWAYML GENERATION TIPS

### **For Best Results:**

1. **Generate in Chunks:**
   - Create 5-10 second clips per prompt
   - Stitch together in editing software
   - Allows for better control and iteration

2. **Use Image-to-Video:**
   - Generate key frames first (use screenshots or Midjourney)
   - Then use RunwayML's image-to-video for motion
   - More control over composition

3. **Text-to-Video Settings:**
   - Duration: 5-10 seconds per clip
   - Aspect Ratio: 16:9
   - Motion: "Medium" or "High" depending on scene energy
   - Seed: Save good seeds to maintain consistency

4. **Consistency Tips:**
   - Use consistent character descriptions across shots
   - Reference previous generations in prompts
   - Keep color palette and lighting descriptions consistent
   - Use "cinematic" and "professional" in all prompts

5. **Upscale & Enhance:**
   - Generate at highest available resolution
   - Use RunwayML's upscaling for final quality
   - Apply subtle film grain in post for cinematic feel

## üöÄ PRODUCTION WORKFLOW

1. **Pre-Production (1 day):**
   - Generate key frame images in Midjourney/DALL-E
   - Storyboard layout with timing
   - Select music tracks

2. **Generation (2-3 days):**
   - Generate all scenes in RunwayML (20-30 clips)
   - Review and regenerate weak shots
   - Upscale final selections

3. **Post-Production (1-2 days):**
   - Edit in Premiere/DaVinci Resolve/CapCut
   - Add music and sound design
   - Color grade for consistency
   - Add text overlays and graphics
   - Render final video

4. **Review & Polish (1 day):**
   - Team review
   - Minor adjustments
   - Final export and upload

**Total Time: 5-7 days for cinematic quality**
